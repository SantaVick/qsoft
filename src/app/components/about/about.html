About Us

Qsoft Limited was incorporated in 2013 and is headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. The founder members of Qsoft have over 60+ years of combined experience in the IT industry, telecommunications, and industrial solutions in the public and private sector. Qsoft delivers IT solutions, industrial automation systems, security solutions, and analytics to its corporate and individual clients.

We provide solutions in analytics, fintech solutions, mobile and web-based applications, security hardware, and software solutions.
